Internship Terms & Conditions

Please review the following terms before starting your internship at Byteproc. These ensure a smooth and professional experience for both interns and the organization.

1.Eligibilty:

Applicants must be students or recent graduates with relevant academic background. All submitted information must be accurate and verifiable.

2.Internship Duration:

Internship durations range from 1 to 6 months. The duration will be confirmed in your selection email and may vary based on domain and role.

3. Code of Conduct

  • Maintain professionalism and respect in all communications.
  • Avoid plagiarism or misuse of project resources.
  • Be punctual, responsive, and committed throughout the internship.

4. Stipend & Certificates

  • Stipends (if applicable) will be shared post-completion via UPI/bank transfer.
  • Certificate of Completion will only be given after fulfilling all internship tasks and submitting final deliverables.

5. Confidentiality

Interns must not disclose, reproduce or share any project information outside Byteproc without written consent. Breach of confidentiality may result in termination.

6. Termination Policy

Byteproc reserves the right to terminate the internship if the intern fails to comply with the terms, misses deadlines, or behaves inappropriately.

Final Agreement

By proceeding with the application, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to abide by the above internship terms and conditions.

We encourage all applicants to carefully review each point for a smooth internship experience.
